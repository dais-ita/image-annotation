Congestion Image Annotation Tool

Congestion is: a line of traffic that would cause you to slow or stop

LEFT arrow: NO CONGESTION
RIGHT arrow: CONGESTION
DOWN arrow: DON'T KNOW
UP arrow: BROKEN IMAGE*
Letter 'u': UNDO last annotation
*(please also use BROKEN for images where the camera cannot see road - e.g. images with the camera pointing at the wall)
